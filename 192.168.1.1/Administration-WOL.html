    Wake-on-LAN (WOL):

    This page allows you to Wake Up hosts on your local network.
    You can manually wake up hosts by clicking the Wake Up button or alternatively by programing an automatic wake up schedule provided by the WOL Daemon.

    MAC Address(es):

    MAC address(es) are entered in the format e.g. 01:23:45:67:89:AB and must be separated by a SPACE.

    IP Address:

    The IP address is typically the broadcast address for the local network, it could also be a remote address when e.g. the target host is not a LAN client.
